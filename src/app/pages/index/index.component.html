Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay to vote?

Not necessarily. You can play for free. But keep in mind that for winning you will receive a cash prize only if you participate in the cash game.

How high are stakes?

The game ticket price starts as low as $1 per game. You can choose the game bet from the offered options. There are also games for free.

How can I win cash?

Each game involves a certain number of participants, who each buy a ticket. Each game is won by one participant, who is paid the sum of the ticket contributions of all participants. VOTERY retains the commission from the total amount of game tickets.

How can I withdraw my winnings?

You can transfer your winnings to your bank account at any time as soon as it reaches the minimum amount of $10.

Do I have to know math to be competitive?

To participate in the game you need to know arithmetic at the first class level. You must be able to add and subtract two-digit integers. However, you can calculate possible next moves and combinations of other players. How precisely and how deep, depends on the sharpness of your mind!

Is my money safe?

Yes. Our system is secure, protected and we use both EU security requirements and qualified payment partners.

Which game should I choose?

All VOTERY games are based on one principle - a combination of numbers and voting for them. Games differ in nuances. Explore the description of each game to make a choice!

How long does it take to play one game?

The game takes place in real time. One game lasts 20 minutes. You can join the game soon to start or one already in progress. The game cannot be postponed or paused.

Is it gambling?

No. This is not gambling. There are no robotic algorithms, lucky numbers or any other random principle. The result of the game is affected only by people who play against each other.
